About Me

My Story and Vision

My Story

Above you can see one of the very first photos I ever took. I had just received the brand-new iPhone XR with the cutting edge face ID and nice camera, and I was eager to try it out.

From that moment I looked at what I had taken, I knew photography was something I wanted to pursue. If I told 13 year old me that I would be taking pictures for numerous organizations, his head would probably explode!

My Vision

My ultimate goal is to capture the world how I see it to share it with others. I think there is something to be captured in every moment we live.

I am so grateful that the world has given me so many opportunities to capture its beauty, and I look forward to every new chance I get to break out my camera.
